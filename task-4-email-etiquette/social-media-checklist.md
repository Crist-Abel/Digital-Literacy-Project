# Social Media Checklist for College Students

##  Do's
- Think before you post and ensure your content is appropriate.
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in all interactions.
- Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Build a positive online presence that reflects your goals and values.

##  Don'ts
- Do not share offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Avoid oversharing personal details like address, phone number, or passwords.
- Do not engage in online arguments or toxic discussions.
- Avoid posting content that could harm your academic or professional reputation.
- Do not blindly trust or share unverified news or links.
